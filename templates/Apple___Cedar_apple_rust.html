Treatment :

Cedar apple rust is a fungal disease that can affect apple trees, causing yellow spots on the leaves and fruit. Here are some steps for cedar apple rust treatment:

Remove nearby cedar trees: Cedar apple rust requires both an apple tree and a cedar tree to complete its life cycle. Removing nearby cedar trees can help reduce the risk of infection.

Apply fungicides: There are several fungicides available for cedar apple rust treatment, including copper-based fungicides and synthetic fungicides. Follow the manufacturer's instructions carefully for application rates and timing.

Suggested Pesticide :

Some common pesticide used to treat cedar apple rust include, Captan, Mancozeb and Sulfur.
