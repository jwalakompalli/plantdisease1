Treatment :

Orange trees affected by Huanglongbing (HLB), also known as Citrus greening, have no known cure. However, there are management strategies that can help to reduce the spread and impact of the disease.

One of the key management strategies is to control the Asian citrus psyllid, which is the insect vector that spreads the bacterium responsible for HLB. This can be done through a combination of cultural practices such as removing infected trees, scouting for psyllids and treating with insecticides when necessary, and using biological control methods such as introducing natural enemies of the psyllid.

Suggested Pesticide :

Some common pesticide used to treat orange huanglongbing include, Oxytetracycline and Streptomycin.
