Treatment :

Esca is a fungal disease that affects grapevines, causing black or brown spots on the leaves, and eventual death of the vine. Here are some steps for grape Esca (black measles) treatment.

Use pruning to reduce infections: Prune grapevines carefully to reduce the number of potential sites for infection. Cut out all diseased wood and prune out crossing branches, which can create wounds that are susceptible to infection.

Use trunk injection treatments: Inject fungicides directly into the trunk of the grapevine to help prevent and control Esca. This is a particularly effective treatment for grapevines that are already infected with the disease.

Suggested Pesticide :

Some common pesticide used to treat grape esca (Black Measles ) include, Boscalid, Copper based fungicides and Triazole fungicides.
