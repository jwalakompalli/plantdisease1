Grape black rot is a fungal disease that can affect grapevines, causing brown or black spots on the fruit and leaves. Here are some steps for grape black rot treatment:

Apply fungicides: There are several fungicides available for grape black rot treatment, including copper-based fungicides and synthetic fungicides. Follow the manufacturer's instructions carefully for application rates and timing.
