Apple scab is a fungal disease that affects apple trees, causing spots and blemishes on the fruit and leaves. Here are some steps to take for apple scab treatment:

Prune the tree: Prune your apple tree to promote good air circulation and sunlight penetration, which can help reduce the spread of the disease.
